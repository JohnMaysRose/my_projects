Antique

My fabel for junk.

Reduce, Reuse, Recycle. Hier is an example.

Material:

- Asus laptop M3N from November 2003 with Lubuntu 18, adb, scrcpy and gnirehtet (optional, you can connect over wifi if you prefer).

- Motorola Moto G from November 2013, developer options on, USB debugging on, with lineage and gnirehtet.

- The charging cable from Moto G (USB-A/ USB-B micro).

Result:

I use the laptop as a network source as well as a "magnifier" for the phone's display. YouTube videos or video calls were not possible anymore. Combining the small, but more powerfull CPU as well as the camera, the microphone and the speakers from the moto G, is now possible to watch videos and make phone calls. For a better experience you can attatch the cell phone to your computer with sugru or similar.
